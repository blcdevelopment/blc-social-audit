What this section tells you

Search Console shows how people already find the site in Google. It highlights pages with visibility but weak clicks, search terms close to stronger rankings, and pages losing traffic. These numbers come directly from Google, not from estimates.

How to read the columns

Impressions = how many times the page appeared in Google results. Clicks = how many times searchers chose it. CTR (click-through rate) = clicks divided by impressions; high impressions with low CTR means the page is seen but not chosen. Position = the average ranking spot in the results (1 is the top).
